Spooky Reads

Coraline

Neil Gaiman

The day after Coraline moves into a new house, she discovers a passage to an alternate world.

The Shining

Stephen King

Story of Jack Torrance's new job at the Overlook Hotel as the off-season caretaker.

Scary Stories to Tell in the Dark

Alvin Schwartz

Collection of some of the most alarming tales of horror, dark revenge, and supernatural events of all time.

Interview with the Vampire

Anne Rice

Gothic horror novel that follows the story of vampire Louis de Pointe du Lac, which he shares with a reporter.

The Devil in the White City

Erik Larson

The true tale of a serial killer who used the 1893 World's Fair to lure his victims to their death.

Dead Until Dark

Charlaine Harris

Inspired HBO's True Blood; story of mind-reader Sookie Stackhouse and her vampire-boyfriend, Bill.

Dracula

Bram Stoker

During a business visit to Count Dracula's castle in Transylvania, a young English solicitor finds himself at the center of a series of horrifying incidents.

The Silence of the Lambs

Thomas Harris

While searching for a serial murderer, Clarice Starling must visit a man confined to a high-security facility for the criminally insane and interview him.

The Haunting of Hill House

Shirley Jackson

The greatest haunted house story ever written, the inspiration for a 10-part Netflix series directed by Mike Flanagan and starring Michiel Huisman, Carla Gugino, and Timothy Hutton.

Heart-Shaped Box

Joe Hill

Judas Coyne is a collector of the macabre. But nothing he possesses is as dreadful the one-of-a-kind curiosity that arrives at his door in a black heart-shaped box...
